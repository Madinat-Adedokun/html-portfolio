About Me


With a robust background in nursing, I have dedicated several years to providing compassionate care
and support to patients in various healthcare settings. My experience has honed my skills in communication,
problem-solving, and teamwork, all of which are essential in both nursing and any collaborative environment.

Recently, I embarked on a new journey into the world of web development, where I discovered a profound passion for
creating digital solutions that enhance user experiences. Through self-directed learning and hands-on projects,
I have developed a solid foundation in programming languages, design principles, and problem-solving techniques.
This exciting transition has not only expanded my skill set but has also invigorated my professional aspirations.

As a mother of two, I understand the importance of balance and adaptability. My family inspires me to
strive for continuous growth, both personally and professionally. I cherish opportunities to travel,
as they enrich my perspective and creativity, allowing me to bring fresh ideas into my work.

I am excited to merge my nursing expertise with my newfound love for web development, contributing
to projects that make a meaningful impact. I look forward to collaborating with others
who share my enthusiasm for innovation and creativity in technology.
